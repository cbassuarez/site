Sebastian Suarez-Solis (b. 1999) is a Caracas-born Venezuelan-American sonic and visual artist (they/them) whose work ranges from musical composition to visual pieces, installations, performances, happenings, and artist-built cybernetic systems.

Their dialectic output is rooted in the ablation of established performance practice, introducing spontaneity, irreverence, and profane action wherever possible, as in the prepared harpsichord solo Seven Sounds for Strings.

Recent cybernetic projects include let go / letting go, THE TUB, String, and Praetorius.
