WORK 1 — String Quartet No. 2 “SOUNDNOISEMUSIC”

A through-composed/indeterminate quartet that alternates fixed score, structured mischief, and noise permissions.

A large-scale quartet that toggles between rigor and chaos. Structured movements, improvisatory noise passages, and performer subversions are interleaved to question how fixed notation and indeterminate practice coexist in chamber music.

WORK 2 — Organum Quadruplum “Lux Nova”

A stained-glass acoustics study: bowed dalle-de-verre slab transduced into a ring of pianos.

Light-conditioned harmonics from a dalle-de-verre slab are bowed, captured, and redistributed across a circle of pianos so counterpoint arises from distance rather than shared resonance.

WORK 3 — AMPLIFICATIONS I · MARIMBAideefixe

Two prepared pianos as resonators for a 5.0-octave marimba.

Marimbaideefixe extends marimba through dual prepared pianos acting as resonant bodies, blurring solo and ensemble roles into one amplified field.
